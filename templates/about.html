Frequently Asked Questions

Is Split Bill Faster really free?

Yes, absolutely! Split Bill Faster is completely free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe that splitting bills should be accessible to everyone.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculations are 100% accurate. We use precise mathematical algorithms to ensure that every split is calculated correctly, including tax, tip, and rounding adjustments.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely! Split Bill Faster is fully responsive and works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. No app download required - just open it in your browser and start splitting bills.

Yes! You can customize individual amounts for each participant. This is perfect for situations where some people ordered more expensive items or have different dietary preferences.
